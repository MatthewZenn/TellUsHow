Tell Us How

When you solve a problem, it is courteous to document the solution for posterity.

You wouldn't answer someone else's question by bragging that you already solved it. Don't answer your own questions this way either.

❌ Don't

Anon:How do I reassign a variable?

Anon:edit: Nevermind guys, I got it working.

✅ Do

Anon:How do I reassign a variable?

Anon:edit: This worked.var x = 10; x = 15;

Everyone starts from the same point, and someone will probably find themselves facing the same problems that you did. Help them out.
